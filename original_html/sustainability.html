Our Vision

As a family group, RADIANT favours a long view rather than focus exclusively on short-term profit.

We believe in human potential and are keen to foster talent and skills by offering our consumers the most innovative, useful, and sustainable products to support their studies and projects.

Furthermore, we are committed to continuously reducing our environmental footprints throughout the product lifecycle.

Reducing Our Environmental Footprint

Our objective is to revisit our products continuously to optimise their environmental footprints using ecodesign and ecolabelling.

Our L.E.D Programme (Leading Eco Design): We are working to reduce the environmental footprint of our products throughout their lifecycle, from manufacturing of notebooks and diaries, to transport and till the end of life.
